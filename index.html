about jaya Kapil

 
  Jaya  Kapil   is   a  renowned Health  and   Nutrition  coach  from  Delhi,  who
  expertise   in   developing   healthy   weight   loss   programs,   putting    into
  consideration the age, body type,medical condition and other factors of her
  clients.     She   always    encourages    a   scientific   and    process-oriented
  approach rather than short-term unhealthy gimmicks to lose weight.
  She  advocates  an “intuitive eating”  approach  to  help  you reconnect with
  your  body’s natural  signals of  hunger,  fullness  and  satisfaction.  To  make 
  your body finer, she always  suggests  eating protein &   fibre rich food, nuts, 
  fruits  &  vegetables.    She  helps  in  developing   a   diet  plan  schedule in
  accordance with your  healthy  weight  loss  goal  without compromising on 
  your   taste and preference.  In her own words,   “I help people to develop a 
  happy and healthier relationship with food.”
  After her MBA, Jaya imbibed the principles of 'continuous learning' with the
  help of national and  international  training  from  world-renowned  doctors 
  and scientists which made her vision stronger.  She is  honoured  as a  Gold 
  medallist in her profession and guiding others for  a  healthy  and nutritious 
  life. In her own life, Jaya has lost 22 kgs and  30  inches  from  her body and 
  living a  healthy  lifestyle  since  then.  She  believes  that  by  following  her 
  advice many people can leady happy and healthy lifestyle. Her husband is 
  also   a   professional   health   and   nutrition  coach   with   more   than   16
  professional degrees in this field.
  She  also  has  expertise   in  helping  you  build  a  strong  immune  system, 
  stamina,  weight gain,  child’s   health   and   healthy   aging   with   effective
  personalized diet plans, which are easy   and   convenient  to  follow.  In her
  career,   she   conducted   many   workshops   addressing  more   than   25
  thousands  of  individuals  offline  and  online  to guide them about healthy
  nutrition.
  She  is  also associated with world’s no 1  Nutrition  Company  “Herbalife” in 
  the capacity of Executive President from last 14 years.
